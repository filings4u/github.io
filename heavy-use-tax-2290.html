Knowledge Base

Common Questions

What is Form 2290?

The Heavy Highway Vehicle Use Tax is a federal tax for vehicles with a taxable gross weight of 55,000 pounds or more.

What is a Schedule 1?

It is the proof of filing provided by the IRS. You need this to renew your vehicle tags and IRP registration.

When is the deadline?

The annual filing season runs from July 1 to June 30. Taxes must be filed by August 31st each year.

Can I file for a suspended vehicle?

Yes. If you expect to drive the vehicle less than 5,000 miles (7,500 for agricultural), you can file for a tax suspension.
