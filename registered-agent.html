Common Questions

What exactly is a Registered Agent?

A Registered Agent is a person or entity authorized to receive legal documents (Service of Process) and official government correspondence on behalf of your business.

Can I be my own Registered Agent?

Technically, yes—but you must list your physical address on public record and be available at that address during all business hours. Most business owners prefer the privacy of a professional service.

What happens if I don't have an agent?

Your business can lose its "Good Standing" status, face administrative dissolution by the state, and you might miss critical notice of a lawsuit.
