Knowledge Base

Common Questions

What is an MC Number?

Operating Authority (MC number) is required for companies that transport passengers or federally regulated commodities in interstate commerce.

How long is the protest period?

Once filed, the FMCSA mandates a 10-day protest period before your authority can be granted.

Do I need insurance first?

You can file for your MC number before getting insurance, but you must have it active within 20 days of filing.

What is a BOC-3?

It is a federal filing that designates process agents in every state in case of legal action against your fleet.
