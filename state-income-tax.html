Knowledge Base

Common Questions

What is an LLC?

A Limited Liability Company protects your personal assets from business debts and lawsuits by creating a separate legal entity.

Do I need an EIN?

Yes, an Employer Identification Number is required to open a business bank account and hire employees. We include this in our Elite and Enterprise plans.

What is a Registered Agent?

A Registered Agent is a designated individual or entity that receives official legal documents on behalf of your business.

How long does formation take?

State processing times vary, but most LLCs are approved within 3-5 business days. Expedited options are available in most states.
