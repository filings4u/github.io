Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 15, 2026

filings4u, LLC ("we," "our," or "us"), a subsidiary of Roseland Companies, LLC, operates the website filings4u.com and its secure client portal. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, protect, use, and share information from users ("you" or "your") purchasing corporate formations, tax filings, and DOT/fleet compliance services.

1. Information Collection

We process data inputs to establish organizational registration maps with regulatory bodies:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, mailing address, telephone number, and account passwords.
  • Corporate and Fleet Data: Business entity names, Employer Identification Numbers (EIN), DOT/MC authorities, vehicle configurations, and fleet parameters.
  • Payment Records: Credit card metadata and billing identifiers processed securely via the Stripe network shadow DOM tree elements. We do not store unencrypted card numbers inside local database clusters.
  • Technical Access Tracking: IP addresses, operating system specifications, browser properties, and workspace navigation activity cached through Supabase data loops.

2. Use of Data

Your profiles populate strict corporate lifecycle automation routines:

  • Statutory Submissions: Relaying operational application datasets to state secretaries, the IRS, and the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration (FMCSA).
  • Multi-Channel Notifications: Transmitting operational system alerts, compliance audits, and renewal indicators to your dashboard and verified mailbox via the Resend API channel.
  • Platform Management: Maintaining user session tokens, calculating business platform metrics, and optimizing data array pipelines inside your portal view.

3. Information Sharing and Third Parties

We do not lease, trade, or distribute your data assets to marketing aggregators. Information is shared strictly with regulatory networks to complete your requested filings:

  • Government Jurisdictions: State filing departments, county registers, the IRS, and the Department of Transportation (DOT).
  • Infrastructure Contractors: Stripe for secure payment processing and Supabase for cloud database architectures.

4. Data Security

All information moving between your terminal and our infrastructure runs over a 256-bit SSL encrypted connection. Data properties are protected by row-level security (RLS) policies within our cloud storage structure to block unauthorized profile matching attempts.
